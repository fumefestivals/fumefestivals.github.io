Frequently Asked Questions

You have questions and we have the answers! You can find some right below here.

I lost personal belongings during the event, where can I find them?
No problem, after each event, found smartphones, wallets, ID cards... are collected and stored safely. Our cleaning team will hand them over to the club. It is therefore best to contact the respective club directly. In case of an outdoor event, you are welcome to send us an email ;)
How do I get the photos and videos of the photographers?
For all our events we hire several photographers and videographers to capture your best moments! To download the best pics, click here to go to the overview.
Is there a shuttle service or are nightliners available?
Depending on the type of event and the date, our transport offer varies. If nightliners are available, this will be communicated in advance on our social media and here on the website. Shuttles are always available through our partner "Shuttle Finder". Download the app for Android and iOS.
How can I become a Fume Promoter?
Our promoters are carefully selected to ensure quality sales and customer support. Feel free to sign up for our waiting list using this form. We look forward to welcoming you soon! Tip: The better your profile looks and the more active you are, the higher is your chance of becoming a promoter ;)
Where can I get tickets and how much do they cost?
Depending on the event, tickets are sold online (Eventbrite) and/or in physical advance sales. You can find the promoters who sell tickets on our event pages and on Instagram. In addition, our partner stores represent us in the same way. For detailed information, please check the event page.
